Design of a Signal Conditioning circuit to detect Fractional Order elements using sensors

A signal conditioning circuit is used after a sensor to prepare its raw output for further processing by devices like analog-to-digital converters or microcontrollers. It performs several key functions, including amplification of weak signals to a usable voltage range, and filtering to remove unwanted noise or interference. Additionally, it can linearize nonlinear sensor outputs, shift signal levels to match input requirements, and even convert signals from one form to another, such as current to voltage
